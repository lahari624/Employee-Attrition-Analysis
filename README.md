#  Employee Attrition Analysis

##  Project Overview

Employee attrition is a critical issue for organizations as it impacts productivity, costs, and team stability.
This project analyzes an HR dataset to identify the **key factors that influence employee attrition** using data analysis and visualization techniques.

The goal of this project is to help organizations **understand employee behavior and improve retention strategies** through data-driven insights.

---

##  Dataset Information

The dataset contains HR-related employee information including:

* Age
* Department
* Job Role
* Monthly Income
* Education
* Job Satisfaction
* Work-Life Balance
* Years at Company
* Attrition Status

The dataset is used to analyze patterns and identify the **main drivers of employee attrition**.

##  Project Workflow

The analysis was completed through the following steps:

1. **Data Collection**

   * Imported HR Employee Attrition dataset.

2. **Data Cleaning**

   * Checked for missing values.
   * Verified data types.
   * Removed inconsistencies.

3. **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)**

   * Analyzed employee demographics.
   * Examined attrition distribution.
   * Identified correlations between variables.

4. **Data Visualization**

   * Attrition by department
   * Attrition by job role
   * Monthly income vs attrition
   * Work-life balance impact

5. **Insights Generation**

   * Identified the major factors affecting employee attrition.

---

##  Key Insights

* Employees with **low job satisfaction** show higher attrition rates.
* Employees with **lower monthly income** are more likely to leave.
* **Work-life balance** plays a significant role in employee retention.
* Some **departments and job roles** show higher attrition levels.
